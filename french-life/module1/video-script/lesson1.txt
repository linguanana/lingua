👋 General Goodbyes
Au revoir – Goodbye (standard and polite)
Salut – Bye (informal, also means "hi")

🫶 Friendly / Unspecified Reunions
À bientôt – See you soon (unspecified time)
À très vite – See you very soon! (enthusiastic)

🕒 Later Today
À tout à l’heure – See you later (today)
À plus tard – See you later today (casual/emphasized)

📅 Next Time / Scheduled
À demain – See you tomorrow
À la prochaine – Until next time
À la semaine prochaine – See you next week

👀 Keeping in Touch
On se voit – See you (in person)
On se parle – Talk to you soon (text or call)

🌞 Polite Wishes
Bonne journée ! – Have a good day
Bonne soirée ! – Have a good evening

{{pause=1000}}Au revoir{{pause=1000}}
A standard goodbye — polite and formal. Like when leaving a shop or hotel.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}Salut !{{pause=1000}}
Hi or bye — friendly and casual. Use it with friends, like saying “Hey!” or “See ya!”{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À bientôt{{pause=1000}}
See you soon — maybe in a few days. Not right away, but not too far either.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À très vite{{pause=1000}}
Very soon — like “See you super soon!” when you can’t wait to meet again.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À tout à l’heure{{pause=1000}}
See you later today — like when leaving for a walk but coming back soon.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À plus tard{{pause=1000}}
Catch you later — casual and open-ended, maybe later today, maybe not.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À demain{{pause=1000}}
See you tomorrow — when you know you’ll meet the next day, like after class or work.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À la prochaine{{pause=1000}}
Until next time — relaxed and friendly, when you don’t know exactly when you’ll meet again.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}À la semaine prochaine{{pause=1000}}
See you next week — a perfect phrase to end a Friday meeting or class.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}On se voit{{pause=1000}}
See you around — casual and relaxed, when you’ll likely cross paths again.{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}On se parle{{pause=1000}}

{{pause=1000}}Bonne journée{{pause=1000}}

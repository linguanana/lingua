********** EN Script (talking script) **********
{{pause=500}}“Piacere!” {{pause=500}}
means “Nice to meet you!” {{pause=500}} It’s short and friendly. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Piacere di conoscerti” {{pause=500}}
is the full, informal version. {{pause=500}} Say it to someone your age or in casual settings. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Piacere di conoscerla” {{pause=500}}
is formal. For example:
When meeting your boss, a teacher, someone older, or during an interview, {{pause=500}}
you can say {{pause=500}}“Piacere di conoscerla” {{pause=500}} to show respect. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Piacere mio” {{pause=500}}
means “The pleasure is mine.” {{pause=500}} It’s a polite way to respond after someone says {{pause=500}}“Piacere.” {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Anche a me” {{pause=500}}
means “Me too.” {{pause=500}} You can also say this right after someone says {{pause=500}}“Piacere.” {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Come si scrive?” {{pause=500}}
means “How do you spell it?” — use it if you didn’t catch their name clearly. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=500}}“Che bel nome!” {{pause=500}}
means “What a nice name!” {{pause=500}}

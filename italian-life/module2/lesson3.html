🇮🇹 Module 2 – Lesson 3: Richieste Speciali

🎬 Theme: How to ask for modifications or specific preparations for your food and drinks. (如何要求餐點飲品的調整或特定做法)
📚 Lessons: 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5

Start with Level 1 — come back for Level 2 or/and 3 as you progress.

✅ Level 1 (A0): Basic Modifications & Sugar

🔐 Key Phrases

Caldo – Hot (熱的)
Freddo – Cold (冷的)
Con latte – With milk (加牛奶)
Senza latte – Without milk (不加牛奶)
Con zucchero – With sugar (加糖)
Senza zucchero – Without sugar (不加糖)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧸: Un caffè caldo, per favore.
→ A hot coffee, please.（請來一杯熱咖啡）

👨‍🍳: Certo!
→ Of course!（當然！）

🧸: Vorrei un tè freddo, senza zucchero.
→ I'd like a cold tea, without sugar.（我想要一杯冰茶，不加糖）

👨‍🍳: Con latte?
→ With milk?（要加牛奶嗎？）

🧸: Senza latte, grazie.
→ Without milk, thank you.（不加牛奶，謝謝）

🧸: Un cappuccino con zucchero, per favore.
→ A cappuccino with sugar, please.（請來一杯加糖的卡布奇諾）

👨‍🍳: Subito!
→ Right away!（馬上好！）

🧸: Un'acqua naturale, fredda.
→ A still water, cold.（一杯冷的礦泉水）

👨‍🍳: Va bene.
→ Okay.（好的）

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice ordering a "cold juice with sugar" and a "hot coffee without milk."

📌 Tip

💠 Italians usually prefer hot drinks, even coffee.

• (義大利人通常偏好熱飲，包括咖啡)

💠 "Latte" means milk in mosr Europen countries, not latte (coffee + mlik) in US

• (Latte是牛奶。單說Latte會得到純牛奶)

💠 Italians often take their coffee without sugar, or add it themselves.

• (義大利人常喝無糖咖啡，或自己加糖)

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Specific Preparations & Dietary Needs

🔐 Key Phrases

Più – More (多一點)
Meno – Less (少一點)
Da portare via – To take away / To go (外帶)
Più caldo – Hotter (更熱的)
Doppio – Double (雙份)
Doppio espresso – Double espresso (雙份濃縮咖啡)
Senza – without (無)
Senza glutine – Gluten-free (無麩質)
lattosio: "Lactose"
Senza lattosio – Lactose-free (無乳糖)
Latte di soia – Soy milk (豆漿)
Latte d'avena – Oat milk (燕麥奶)
Sono allergico/a a... – I am allergic to... (我對...過敏)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧸: Vorrei un cappuccino con meno schiuma.
→ I'd like a cappuccino with less foam.（我想要一杯少點奶泡的卡布奇諾）

👨‍🍳: Ok, e lo vuole più caldo?
→ Okay, and do you want it hotter?（好的，要更熱一點嗎？）

🧸: Sì, per favore. E un doppio espresso.
→ Yes, please. And a double espresso.（是的，麻煩了。還有一份雙份濃縮咖啡）

🧸: Avete qualcosa senza glutine?
→ Do you have anything gluten-free?（你們有無麩質的嗎？）

👨‍🍳: Sì, abbiamo dei biscotti senza glutine.
→ Yes, we have some gluten-free biscuits.（是的，我們有幾種無麩質餅乾）

🧸: Posso avere un caffè con latte d'avena? Sono allergica alle noci.
→ Can I have a coffee with oat milk? I am allergic to nuts.（我可以來一杯燕麥奶咖啡嗎？我對堅果過敏）

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice ordering a "lactose-free latte to go" and a "hotter double espresso."

📌 Tip

💠 Avete = you have

• Io ho =I have
• Tu hai = You [singular] have
• Lui/Lei ha = He/She has
> • Voi aveted = You [plural] have)
• Noi abbiamo = we have
• Loro hanno = They have

💠 qualcosa = "something" / anything

💠 Dei= some/any

💠 Biscotti = cookies

💠 e lo vuole più caldo?

• e = And
• lo = it (referring to the drink)
• vuole = do you (formal) want
• più caldo = hotter (literally "more hot")

💠 Più means "more," Meno means "less." go before the adjective or noun

• (Più是「多」，Meno是「少」。放在修飾詞或名詞前)

💠 "Da portare via" = "to carry away." Takeaway options are less common in Italy

• (Da portare via直譯為「帶走」。義大利傳統Bar外帶較不常見)

💠 For allergies,

Allergico is for males,

allergica for females.

💠 Alternative milks (like soy, oat) are getting more common in bigger cities.

• (替代奶（豆漿、燕麥奶）越來越常見，大城市尤其多)

💠 Understanding useful words in Level 2 phrases:

• allergico/a: "Allergic" (male/female form)
• a: "To" (preposition)

✅ Level 3 (A1): Polite Requests & Specific Items

🔐 Key Phrases

panna: "Cream" (often whipped cream)
cannuccia: "Straw"
scaldarlo: "To warm it up" (scaldare - to warm, -lo - it)
un po': "A little bit"
Mi scusi: "Excuse me / Sorry"
Mi può: "Can you (for me)"
portare: "To bring"
È possibile: "Is it possible"
extra: "Extra" (can be used in Italian as well)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧸: Vorrei un caffè con panna.
→ I'd like a coffee with cream.（我想要一杯加鮮奶油的咖啡）

🧸: Mi scusi, posso avere una cannuccia?
→ Excuse me, can I have a straw?（不好意思，我可以要一根吸管嗎？）

👨‍🍳: Certo!
→ Certainly!（當然！）

🧸: Questo è un po' freddo. Mi può scaldarlo un po'?
→ This is a bit cold. Can you warm it up a little for me?（這個有點冷。可以幫我稍微加熱嗎？）

👨‍🍳: Subito!
→ Right away!（馬上好！）

🧸: È possibile avere del ghiaccio extra?
→ Is it possible to have extra ice?（可以給我額外的冰塊嗎？）

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice asking politely: "Excuse me, can you bring me a glass of water?" (Mi scusi, mi può portare un bicchiere d'acqua?)

📌 Tip

💠 Mi scusi = "excuse me" (to get attention) or "I'm sorry."

• (Mi scusi是多用途禮貌語，表示「不好意思」或「抱歉」)

💠 Mi può portare...? a very polite way to ask for something to be brought to you.

• 是非常禮貌地請對方拿東西給你的方式)

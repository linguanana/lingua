✅ Level 1 (A0): What is "Il Bar"? & Basic Coffee Types

🔐 Key Phrases

Il Bar – Italian coffee shop (義式咖啡館)
Un caffè – An espresso (濃縮咖啡)
Un cappuccino – A cappuccino (卡布奇諾)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑‍🏫 When: Ordering your first Italian coffee. (第一次點咖啡)
Say Un caffè, per favore. – "An espresso, please." (請來濃縮咖啡)

🧑‍🏫 When: You'd like a classic milk and coffee drink (morning). (早上想喝奶咖)
Say Un cappuccino, per favore. – "A cappuccino, please." (請來卡布奇諾)

🤖 Task or Homework

Imagine ordering in a bar. Try saying Un caffè, per favore.

📌 Tip

💠 In Italy, a Bar is for coffee, pastries, and light meals. (Bar在義大利是咖啡館)

- Cappuccino is usually a breakfast drink. (卡布奇諾通常是早餐飲品)

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Ordering and Understanding Responses

🔐 Key Phrases

Vorrei... – I would like... (我想要...)
Subito! – Right away! (馬上好！)
Prego! – Here you go! / You're welcome! (請 / 給您！)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑‍🏫 When: You want to be polite when ordering. (點餐更禮貌)
Say Vorrei un caffè, per favore. – "I'd like an espresso, please." (我想要濃縮咖啡)

🧑‍🏫 When: The barista confirms your order. (咖啡師確認訂單)
Listen for "Subito!" – "Right away!" (馬上好！)

🧑‍🏫 When: The barista hands you your drink. (咖啡師遞飲品)
Listen for "Prego!" – "Here you go!" (請 / 給您)

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice ordering a cappuccino using Vorrei....

📌 Tip

💠 pay at the cashier first, then show your receipt to the barista. (先結帳再取咖啡)

- Look for "Cassa" (cashier,結帳處)

✅ Level 3 (A1): Drinking Styles & Price Differences

🔐 Key Phrases

Al banco – At the counter (在櫃台)
Al tavolo – At the table (在桌邊)
Servizio al tavolo – Table service fee (桌邊服務費)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑‍🏫 When: You want to sit down, but are unsure about the price. (坐下前，確認價格)
Say "È lo stesso prezzo al tavolo?" – "Is it the same price at the table?" (桌邊價格一樣嗎？)

🧑‍🏫 When: You want to directly ask about a service fee. (直接問服務費)
Say "C'è il servizio al tavolo?" – "Is there table service (fee)?" (有桌邊服務費嗎？)

🤖 Task or Homework

Imagine the price difference between al banco and al tavolo.

📌 Tip

💠 al banco, at the counter is typically cheaper.(站在櫃台喝較便宜)

💠 Al tavolo, at a table often incurs a fee (table service). (桌邊坐通常加收服務費)

💠 Check banco and tavolo prices in the menu (入座前請看菜單價格)

È = is

lo stesso = the same

prezzo = price

C'è = is there

servizio = service (fee)

✅ Extra: Understand Coffee(s)

Beyond Espresso and Cappuccino, Italy offers a rich variety of coffee drinks.
Here's a guide to help you understand them better

Popular Italian Coffee Types (常見義式咖啡種類)

Caffè Lungo – "Long Coffee" (加水濃縮咖啡)
Espresso + more hot water.

Caffè Ristretto – "Restricted Coffee" (少量濃縮咖啡)
Very short espresso (less water, intense)

Caffè Macchiato – "Stained Coffee" (少量奶泡濃縮咖啡)
Espresso + dash of frothed milk.

Latte Macchiato – "Stained Milk" (牛奶中加咖啡)
Steamed milk + espresso (layered, more milk)

Caffè Latte – "Coffee Milk" (義式拿鐵)
Espresso + hot milk (large cup). (Note: 'Latte' alone is plain milk!)

Caffè Americano – "American Coffee" (美式咖啡)
Espresso + large amount of hot water.

Caffè Corretto – "Corrected Coffee" (加烈酒咖啡)
Espresso + shot of liquor

Caffè Shakerato – "Shaken Coffee" (冰搖咖啡)
Espresso + ice + (sugar), shaken cold

Caffè con Panna – "Coffee with Cream" (加鮮奶油咖啡)
Espresso + whipped cream

Affogato – "Drowned" (咖啡淋冰淇淋)
Vanilla ice cream + hot espresso (dessert)

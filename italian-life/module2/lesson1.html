✅ Level 1 (A0): What is "Il Bar"? & Basic Coffee Types

📗 Key Phrases

Il Bar – Italian coffee shop (義式咖啡館)
Un caffè – An espresso (濃縮咖啡)
Un cappuccino – A cappuccino (卡布奇諾)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🍈 When: Ordering your first Italian coffee. (第一次點咖啡)
🧸: Un caffè, per favore. – An espresso, please. (請來濃縮咖啡)
🧸: Un cappuccino, per favore. – A cappuccino, please. (請來卡布奇諾)

🤖 Task or Homework

Imagine ordering in a bar. Try saying Un caffè, per favore.

📌 Tip

💠 In Italy, a Bar is for coffee, pastries, and light meals. (Bar在義大利是咖啡館)

- Cappuccino is usually a breakfast drink. (卡布奇諾通常是早餐飲品)

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Ordering and Understanding Responses

📗 Key Phrases

Vorrei... – I would like... (我想要...)
Subito! – Right away! (馬上好！)
Prego! – Here you go! / You're welcome! (請 / 給您！)

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧸: Vorrei un caffè, per favore. – "I'd like an espresso, please." (我想要濃縮咖啡)
👨‍🍳: "Subito!" – "Right away!" (馬上好！)
👨‍🍳: "Prego!" – "Here you go!" (請 / 給您)

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice ordering a cappuccino using Vorrei....

📌 Tip

💠 pay at the cashier first, then show your receipt to the barista. (先結帳再取咖啡)

- Look for "Cassa" (cashier,結帳處)

✅ Level 3 (A1): Full Interaction at the Bar

📗 Key Phrases:

Posso avere – May I 可以..？
Vorrei anche – I’d also like 我也想要..
Pago con carta. – I’ll pay by card 用信用卡付
Mi consiglia qualcosa? – Can you recommend something (to me)? 推薦
Cosa mi consiglia? - What do you recommend (to me) 推薦
Al banco – At the counter（在吧檯）
Al tavolo – At the table（在桌邊）
Servizio al tavolo – Table service fee（桌邊服務費）
C'è il servizio al tavolo? – is ther table service fee（有桌邊服務費嗎？）

🎯 Dialogue

🧸: Buongiorno! Posso avere un caffè lungo?
→ May I have a long coffee?（我可以來一杯長濃咖啡嗎？）

👨‍🍳: Certo! Lo prende qui o da portare via?
→ Drink here or take away?（內用還是外帶？）

🧸: Qui, grazie.
→ Here, thanks.（內用，謝謝）

🧸: Vorrei anche un cornetto al cioccolato.
→ I’d also like a chocolate croissant.（也想要一個巧克力可頌）

👨‍🍳: Subito. Vuole pagare con carta?
→ Right away. Would you like to pay by card?（馬上來。您要用卡付款嗎？）

🧸: Sì, pago con carta. Ah, mi consiglia qualcosa di dolce?
→ Yes, I’ll pay by card. Can you recommend something sweet?（對，我用信用卡。你能推薦甜點嗎？）

👨‍🍳: Abbiamo tiramisù fresco. È molto buono!
→ We have fresh tiramisù. It’s very good.（我們有新鮮的提拉米蘇，非常好吃）

🧸: Perfetto, ne prendo uno. Grazie!
→ Perfect, I’ll take one. Thanks!（太好了，我要一份。謝謝！）


🍈 When: you’re asked where you’ll drink your coffee.
Say Al banco – At the bar counter. Cheaper and faster.（在櫃台喝，通常比較便宜快速）
Say Al tavolo – At the table. May include service fee.（在桌邊，有時加收服務費）

🤖 Task or Homework

Try saying Lo prendo al banco when you want to drink at the counter.
Try saying Lo prendo al tavolo if you want table service.
Try asking Mi consiglia qualcosa di dolce? at a café.

📌 Tip

💠 Al banco – Standing at the bar. It’s quick and usually cheaper.（站著喝，通常比較便宜）

💠 Al tavolo – Sitting at the table. More relaxed but may cost more.（坐著喝，舒適但可能加費）

- Servizio al tavolo means table service fee.（桌邊服務費）

- ask: C'è il servizio al tavolo? – Is there a table service fee?（有加收服務費？）

✅ Extra: Understand Coffee(s)

Beyond Espresso and Cappuccino, Italy offers a rich variety of coffee drinks.
Here's a guide to help you understand them better


Popular Italian Coffee Types (常見義式咖啡種類)

Caffè Lungo – "Long Coffee" (加水濃縮咖啡)
Espresso + more hot water.

Caffè Ristretto – "Restricted Coffee" (少量濃縮咖啡)
Very short espresso (less water, intense)

Caffè Macchiato – "Stained Coffee" (少量奶泡濃縮咖啡)
Espresso + dash of frothed milk.

Latte Macchiato – "Stained Milk" (牛奶中加咖啡)
Steamed milk + espresso (layered, more milk)

Caffè Latte – "Coffee Milk" (義式拿鐵)
Espresso + hot milk (large cup). (Note: 'Latte' alone is plain milk!)

Caffè Americano – "American Coffee" (美式咖啡)
Espresso + large amount of hot water.

Caffè Corretto – "Corrected Coffee" (加烈酒咖啡)
Espresso + shot of liquor

Caffè Shakerato – "Shaken Coffee" (冰搖咖啡)
Espresso + ice + (sugar), shaken cold

Caffè con Panna – "Coffee with Cream" (加鮮奶油咖啡)
Espresso + whipped cream

Affogato – "Drowned" (咖啡淋冰淇淋)
Vanilla ice cream + hot espresso (dessert)

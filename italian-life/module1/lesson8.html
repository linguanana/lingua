✅ Level 1 (A0): Le domande base – Basic Question Words

📗 Key Phrase

Come – How（怎麼樣）
Chi – Who（誰）
Cosa / Che cosa – What（什麼）
Dove – Where（哪裡）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👧 Come stai?
→ How are you?（你好嗎？）

👧 Chi è lei?
→ Who is she?（她是誰？）

👦 Cosa fai oggi?
→ What are you doing today?（你今天做什麼？）

👧 Dove vai?
→ Where are you going?（你去哪裡？）

👦 Dove è il bagno?
→ Where is the bathroom?（洗手間在哪裡？）

📌 Tip

Come asks about manner or condition, like “how” someone feels.（問「怎麼樣」）

Chi is used to ask about a person.（問人是誰）

Cosa / Che cosa means "what". You can use either.（意思是「什麼」）

Dove is used for locations or destinations.（問「哪裡」）

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Le domande in più – More Question Words

📗 Key Phrase

Quando – When（何時）
Perché – Why（為什麼）
Quale – Which（哪一個）
Quanto / Quanti – How much / How many（多少）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👦 Quando parte il treno?
→ When does the train leave?（火車什麼時候開？）

👩 Perchénon sei venuto stamattina?
→ Why didn’t you come this morning?（你今天早上為什麼沒來？）

👧 Quale gelato vuoi?
→ Which ice cream do you want?（你想要哪一種冰淇淋？）

👦 Quanti amici hai in Italia?
→ How many friends do you have in Italy?（你在義大利有幾個朋友？）

👧 Quanto costa?
→ How much does it cost?（這多少錢？）

📌 Tip

Quando is for asking about time.（問時間）

Perché is for asking the reason.（問原因）

Quale is used when there’s a choice.（問「哪一個」）

Quanto / Quanti depends on gender/number: quanto (masc. sing.), quanti (masc. plural) — ask price or quantity.（問數量或價格）

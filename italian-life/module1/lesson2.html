✅ Level 1 (A0): Ask "How are you?"

🔐 Key Phrase

Come stai? – How are you?（你好嗎？）
Come sta? – How are you? formal（您好嗎？）
Come va? – How’s it going?（最近如何？）
Bene – I’m good / Fine（我很好）
Così così – So-so（普普通通）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👋 When: Greeting a friend casually 朋友間打招呼
Say Come stai? – informal and friendly（口語用法，輕鬆自在）

🧑‍🏫 When: Talking to a teacher 老師或長輩
Say Come sta? – polite version（正式說法）

🤝 When: Meeting someone at a café 咖啡廳見面
Say Come va? – neutral, very common（中性用法）

😊 When: Feeling good 感覺不錯時
Say Bene! – I'm fine（我很好）

😐 When: Feeling okay 普普通通時
Say Così così – so-so（馬馬虎虎）

😟 When: Not feeling great 有點不舒服時
Say Non molto bene – not so good（不太好）

🤖 Task or Homework

Ask someone today: Come stai? and reply with Bene or Così così.

📌 Tip

Sto bene – comes from stare (to be), meaning “I’m well”（我很好，來自 stare）
You can also just reply with Bene.（簡單說 Bene 也可以）
Come va? – very casual, works almost everywhere（常見又自然）

🎬 Theme: Asking how someone is
📚 Lessons: 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7

Start with Level 1 — come back for Level 2 or/and 3 as you progress.

✅ Level 1 (A0): Ask “How are you?”

🔐 Key Phrase

Come stai? – How are you?（你好嗎？）
Come va? – How’s it going?（最近如何？）
Bene – Good（我很好）
Così così – So-so（普普通通）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧒 When: You meet a friend at the market（市場遇見朋友）
Say Come stai? – informal, warm.

🤖 Task or Homework

Greet someone with “Come stai?” and see how they respond.

📌 Tip

Come va? is often used when chatting casually with people you know.

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Use replies with emotions

🔐 Key Phrase

Non molto bene – Not very well（不太好）
Sono stanco/a – I’m tired（我很累）
Che giornata! – What a day!（真是的一天！）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

😩 When: You’re having a hard day（不順的一天）
Say Non molto bene or Che giornata!

🤖 Task or Homework

Try replying “Sono stanco/a” next time someone asks how you are.

📌 Tip

Stanco is for males, stanca is for females.

✅ Level 3 (A1): React & show concern

🔐 Key Phrase

Mi dispiace – I’m sorry to hear that（我很遺憾聽到這個）
Coraggio! – Cheer up!（加油！）
Andrà tutto bene – Everything will be fine（一切都會好）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 When: Someone says they’re not well（對方說他不太好）
Say Mi dispiace or Coraggio! to show empathy.

🤖 Task or Homework

Practice saying “Andrà tutto bene” to encourage a friend or yourself!

📌 Tip

Andrà tutto bene is a common phrase used during tough times to offer hope.

✅ Level 1 (A0): Subject pronouns

📌 Tip

💠 Common usage of subject pronouns:

Italian subject pronouns are usually dropped — verbs show who’s speaking.

But for emphasis or clarity, you can use them (e.g., **Io** sono di Roma).

Use “Tu” informally. Use “Lei” (capitalized) for a formal "you".

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Possessive adjectives

🔐 Key Vocabulary

English Chinese Italian
my我的mio / mia
your你的tuo / tua
his他的suo
her她的sua

📌 Tip

💠 Understanding possessive adjectives:

Possessives agree with the **gender of the thing possessed**, not the owner.
e.g., **la mia casa** (my house), **il mio libro** (my book).

Use a **definite article** (il, la) with possessives most of the time.

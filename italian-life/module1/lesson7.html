🇮🇹 Module 1 – Lesson 7: Saluti nel quartiere (街坊間的問候)

🎬 Theme: Greetings and small talk with neighbors
📚 Lessons: 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7

✅ Level 1 (A0): Say Hello Around the Neighborhood

🔐 Key Phrase

Ciao! – Hi!（嗨）
Buongiorno! – Good morning!（早安）
Buonasera! – Good evening!（晚上好）
Salve! – Hello (neutral/formal)（您好）

🎯 Dialogue 🎧 Listen to the audio


👩 A: Ciao, Maria!
→ Hi, Maria!（嗨，Maria！）

👨 B: Buongiorno, signore!
→ Good morning! Mr.（早安, 先生）

👩 A: Buonasera, signora!
→ Good evening, ma’am!（晚上好，女士！）

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Ask How They Are

🔐 Key Phrase

Come stai? – How are you?（你好嗎）
Tutto bene? – Everything good?（一切都好嗎）
Come va? – How's it going?（最近怎麼樣）
Che si dice? – What's up?（有什麼新鮮事）

🎯 Dialogue 🎧 Listen to the audio


👨 A: Ciao! Come stai?
→ Hi! How are you?（嗨！你好嗎？）

👩 B: Tutto bene, grazie. E tu?
→ All good, thanks. And you?（都好，謝謝。你呢？）

👨 A: Non c'è male!
→ Not bad!（還不錯！）

✅ Level 3 (A1): Small Talk & Friendly Goodbyes

🔐 Key Phrase

Che fai di bello? – What are you up to?（最近在忙什麼）
Ci vediamo! – See you!（再見）
A dopo! – See you later!（待會見）
Buona giornata! – Have a good day!（祝你今天愉快）
Buona serata! – Have a nice evening!（祝你晚上愉快）

🎯 Dialogue 🎧 Listen to the audio


👩 A: Ciao! Che fai di bello?
→ Hi! What are you up to?（嗨～最近怎麼樣？）

👨 B: Sto andando a fare la spesa.
→ I’m going grocery shopping.（我要去買東西）

👩 A: Allora, a dopo! Buona giornata!
→ See you later! Have a good day!（待會見，祝你愉快）

🤖 Task

Try using different greetings when you meet neighbors today. Ask how they are and say goodbye politely.

📌 Tip

“Come stai?” is informal. Use “Come sta?” for formal settings.
“Tutto bene?” is casual and often used with friends.
“A dopo” is used when you expect to see someone again soon.
“Buona giornata” is great for saying goodbye in the morning or afternoon.

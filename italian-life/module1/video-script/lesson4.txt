EN:
{{pause=2000}}“Piacere!”{{pause=2000}}
means “Nice to meet you!” {{pause=500}} it’s short and friendly. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=2000}}“Piacere di conoscerti”{{pause=2000}}
is the full, informal version {{pause=500}} say it to someone your age or in casual settings. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=2000}}“Piacere di conoscerla”{{pause=2000}}
is formal . For example:
When meeting your boss, a teacher, someone older or during interview,
you can say {{pause=1000}}“Piacere di conoscerla”{{pause=1000}} to show respect.

{{pause=2000}}“Anche a me”{{pause=2000}}
means “Me too” — you can say this right after someone says {{pause=1000}}“Piacere.” {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=2000}}“Come si scrive?”{{pause=2000}}
means “How do you spell it?” — use it if you didn’t catch their name clearly. {{pause=1000}}

{{pause=2000}}“Che bel nome!”{{pause=2000}}
means “What a nice name!” {{pause=500}}
It’s a sweet way to give someone a compliment. {{pause=1000}}
If someone tells you their name and you like it, just smile and say {{pause=1000}}“Che bel nome!” 😊{{pause=1000}}

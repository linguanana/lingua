🇸🇮 Module 1 – Lesson 3: Parole di cortesia e richieste gentili

🎬 Theme: Say thank you, excuse me, and ask politely for help or permission
📚 Lessons: 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7 | 8

Start with Level 1 — come back for Level 2 or 3 as you progress.

✅ Level 1 (A0): Basic polite words

📗 Key Phrases

Grazie – Thank you
Prego – You’re welcome / Please go ahead
Per favore – Please
Scusa – Excuse me / Sorry (informal)
Scusi – Excuse me / Sorry (formal)
Mi scusi – Excuse me (very polite)
Di niente – No problem / Don’t mention it
Signore / Signora / Signori – Sir / Ma’am / Ladies and gentlemen

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Requests & understanding

📗 Key Phrases

Capisco / Non capisco – I understand / I don’t understand
Posso...? – Can I...?
Può...? – Can you...? (formal)
Può ripetere, per favore? – Can you repeat, please?
Posso entrare? – Can I come in?
Posso usare il bagno? – Can I use the bathroom?
Non capisco bene l’italiano. – I don’t understand Italian well
Può parlare più lentamente? – Can you speak more slowly?

🌟 Dialogue

👧: Scusi, posso fare una domanda?
→ Excuse me, can I ask a question?

🤹: Certo! Dimmi pure.
→ Of course! Go ahead and ask.

👧: Non capisco... Può ripetere, per favore?
→ I don’t understand… Can you repeat, please?

🤹: Sì! Ripeto lentamente.
→ Yes! I’ll repeat slowly.

👧: Ah! Capisco, grazie mille!
→ Ah! I understand, thank you very much!

🤹: Prego!
→ You’re welcome!

✅ Level 3 (A1): Real-life polite requests

📗 Key Phrases

Vorrei... – I would like...
C'è...? / Ci sono...? – Is there...? / Are there...?
Le / Ti posso aiutare? – Can I help you? (formal / informal)
Certamente / Certo – Certainly / Of course
Volentieri – Gladly / With pleasure
Mi dispiace – I’m sorry
Con permesso – Excuse me (to pass by / interrupt)

🌟 Dialogue

👧: Mi scusi, Signora, posso chiederle un’informazione?
→ Excuse me, Madam, may I ask you for some information?

👩: Certamente, prego!
→ Certainly, please go ahead!

👧: Vorrei comprare un souvenir. C’è un negozio di souvenir qui vicino?
→ I’d like to buy a souvenir. Is there a souvenir shop nearby?

👩: Sì, c’è un negozio lì, a destra.
→ Yes, there’s a shop there, to the right.

👧: Grazie mille!
→ Thank you very much!

👩: Prego!
→ You’re welcome!

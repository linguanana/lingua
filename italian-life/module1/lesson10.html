✅ Level 1 (A0): Basic understanding & requests

📗 Key Phrase

Capisco – I understand（我懂）
Non capisco – I don’t understand（我不懂）
Posso...? – Can I...?（我可以⋯嗎？）
Per favore – Please（請）
Prego – You're welcome / Please go ahead（不客氣／請）
Può ripetere, per favore? – Can you repeat, please?（可以請您再說一次嗎？）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👧: Scusi, posso fare una domanda?
→ Excuse me, can I ask a question?（不好意思，我可以問一個問題嗎？）

🧸‍: Certo! Dimmi pure.
→ Of course! Go ahead and ask.（當然，請問吧。）

👧: Non capisco... Può ripetere, per favore?
→ I don’t understand… Can you repeat, please?（我不懂，可以請您重複嗎？）

🧸: Sì! Ripeto lentamente.
→ Yes! I'll repeat slowly.（好，我慢慢說。）

👧: Ah! Capisco, grazie mille!
→ Ah! I understand. Thank you very much!（啊，我懂了，非常感謝！）

🧸: Prego!
→ You're welcome!（不客氣！）

📌 Tip

💠 Use Capisco when you understand something clearly.

Use Non capisco when you want help or clarification.
Use Posso...? to politely ask permission.
Reply with Prego to say “You’re welcome.”

💠:

Use Posso...? to ask **“Can I...?”**（我可以⋯嗎？）
Use Può...? to ask **“Can you...?”**（您可以⋯嗎？），這是較禮貌或正式的說法。
Add per favore to make your request more polite（加上「請」會更禮貌）
Don’t be afraid to say Non capisco bene – it’s natural when learning!（說「我不太懂」是學習過程的自然反應）

✅ Level 2 (A0+): Everyday polite phrases

📗 Key Phrase

Posso entrare? – Can I come in?（我可以進來嗎？）
Posso usare il bagno? – Can I use the bathroom?（我可以用洗手間嗎？）
Non capisco bene l’italiano. – I don’t understand Italian well.（我不太懂義大利文）
Può parlare più lentamente? – Can you speak more slowly?（你可以說慢一點嗎？）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧸: Scusi, posso usare il bagno?
→ Excuse me, can I use the bathroom?（不好意思，我可以用洗手間嗎？）

👨‍🍳‍: Sì, certo! È in fondo a destra.
→ Yes, of course! It's in the back, to the right.（可以的，在後面右邊。）

🧸: Grazie mille!
→ Thanks a lot!（非常感謝！）

👨‍🍳: Prego!
→ You’re welcome!（不客氣！）

📌 Tip

💠 Posso... is useful in shops, cafes, or daily life.

Use it with actions like usare (to use), entrare (to enter).

Add per favore to sound extra polite.

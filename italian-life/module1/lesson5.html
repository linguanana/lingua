🇮🇹 Module 1 – Lesson 5: Cosa fai? / Che lavoro fai?

🎮 Theme: Ask what someone is doing now and what their job is（現在在做什麼／職業是什麼）
📚 Lessons: 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7

Start with Level 1 — come back for Level 2 as you progress

✅ Level 1 (A0): Ask what someone is doing now

🔐 Key Phrase

Che cosa fai? – What are you doing?（你現在在做什麼？）
Cosa fai? – What are you doing?（你在幹嘛？比較口語）
Studio. – I’m studying.（我在學習）
Lavoro. – I’m working.（我在工作）
Guardo la TV. – I’m watching TV.（我在看電視）
Leggo un libro. – I’m reading a book.（我在讀書）
Cucino. – I’m cooking.（我在做飯）
Mi rilasso. – I’m relaxing.（我在放鬆）
Faccio colazione/pranzo/cena. – I’m having breakfast/lunch/dinner.（我在吃早餐／午餐／晚餐）
Niente (di speciale). – Nothing (special).（沒什麼特別的）
Sto studiando l'italiano. – I’m studying Italian.（我在學習義大利語）
Sto navigando in internet. – I’m surfing the internet.（我在上網）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑 Casual chat at home（在家隨便聊天）
👩 A: Cosa fai?
→ What are you doing?（你在幹嘛？）

👨 B: Cucino. E tu?
→ I’m cooking. And you?（我在做飯，你呢？）

👩 A: Guardo la TV.
→ I’m watching TV.（我在看電視）

👨 C: Faccio cena.
→ I’m making dinner.（我在做晚餐）

🧒 D: Studio l'italiano.
→ I’m studying Italian.（我在學習義大利語）

🧑 E: Navigo in internet.
→ I’m surfing the internet.（我在上網）

👦 F: Niente di speciale.
→ Nothing special.（沒什麼特別的）

📌 Tip

Use "Cosa fai?" for casual questions（非正式用語）
Present tense verbs describe what you’re doing now（用現在式描述）
Many activities don’t require an object: "Studio", "Lavoro"（我學，我工作）
For ongoing actions, use "Sto + gerundio" if needed（用“我正在...”可用 gerundio）
✨ Niente di speciale is a very casual way to say you’re not doing anything important or unusual — similar to "nothing much" or "just chilling"（很口語化，意思是不是在做什麼重要事）

🔹 Level 2 (A0+): Ask and answer about professions

🔐 Key Phrase

Che lavoro fai? – What do you do for work?（你是做什麼的？）
Faccio l'insegnante. – I’m a teacher.（我是老師）
Faccio il medico. – I’m a doctor.（我是醫生）
Lavoro in un ufficio. – I work in an office.（我在辦公室工作）
Sono studente/studentessa. – I’m a student.（我是學生）
Sono disoccupato/a. – I’m unemployed.（我沒有工作）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

🧑 Small talk in class（講課前的小聊）
🧒 A: Che lavoro fai?
→ What do you do?（你是做什麼的？）

👨 B: Lavoro in banca.
→ I work in a bank.（我在銀行工作）

👩 C: Io sono insegnante. E tu?
→ I’m a teacher. And you?（我是老師，你呢？）

🧑 D: Faccio la designer grafica.
→ I’m a graphic designer.（我是幾何設計師）

👦 E: Io sono studente.
→ I’m a student.（我是學生）

📌 Tip

Use "Che lavoro fai?" to ask someone’s job or profession（問對方職業）
"Faccio..." or "Sono..." can both introduce your job（不同語形但都展示職業）
"Cosa fai?" can also mean "What’s your job?" depending on the context（要看語境，也可指職業）

✨ Level 3 (A1): Understand that "Cosa fai?" can mean different things

🔐 Key Phrase

Cosa fai? – What are you doing? / What do you do?（你現在做什麼？／你做什麼工作？）
Che lavoro fai? - what job do you do 你做什麼工作
Faccio l’infermiera. – I’m a nurse.（我是護士）
Lavoro in una scuola. – I work in a school.（我在學校工作）
Sono un ingegnere. – I’m an engineer.（我是工程師）
Sono disoccupata. – I’m unemployed.（我目前沒工作）

🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👩 A: Cosa fai?
→ What do you do?（你是做什麼的？）

👨 B: Sono un ingegnere. E tu?
→ I’m an engineer. And you?（我是工程師，你呢？）

👩 A: Io lavoro in una scuola materna.
→ I work in a preschool.（我在幼兒園工作）

🧑 C: Io sono disoccupata al momento.
→ I’m currently unemployed.（我現在沒在工作）

👧 D: Io studio, non lavoro ancora.
→ I’m studying, not working yet.（我還在唸書，還沒工作）

📌 Tip

"Cosa fai?" can mean "What are you doing now?" or "What do you do for a living?" depending on tone/context（語氣和情境不同，意思不同）
In informal speech, it’s common to ask just "Cosa fai?" without specifying whether it’s about work（日常口語常直接說）
Listen to the situation and respond accordingly（根據語境理解和回答）

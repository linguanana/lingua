🎯 Dialogue / When to use

👩 A: Ciao! Come ti chiami?
→ Hi! What’s your name?（嗨！你叫什麼名字？）

🧸 B: Mi chiamo Marco. E tu?
→ My name is Marco. And you?（我叫 Marco。你呢？）

👩 A: Mi chiamo Laura.
→ My name is Laura.（我叫 Laura。）

👋 Wrapping up a conversation?
Say A dopo! – See you later（待會見）

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Saying goodbye to a group of friends?
Say A dopo ragazzi! – See you later, guys!（大家待會見）

🕒 Meeting again later today?
Say A più tardi! – See you later today（今天稍後見）

🧡 Want to see today’s sweets?
👩‍🦰 Cosa c’è di dolce oggi?
→ What sweet things are there today?（今天有什麼甜點？）

🧡 Want freshly squeezed juice.
👩‍🦰 Vorrei un succo d’arancia spremuta.
→ I’d like a freshly squeezed orange juice.（我想要一杯現榨柳橙汁）
